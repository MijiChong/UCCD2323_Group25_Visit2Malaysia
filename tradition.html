Malay Tradition

Costume

Baju Melayu

Baju melayu is a traditional Malay outfit for men. It consists of a long-sleeved shirt and matching trousers. The shirt usually has a loose fit and features a high collar with a buttoned placket. The trousers are also loose-fitting, allowing for comfort and ease of movement.

Baju Kurung

Baju kurung is a traditional Malay outfit commonly worn by women. It typically consists of a long, loose-fitting blouse and a matching skirt or trousers. The blouse usually has a straight or slightly flared cut and may feature a high neckline with a button-down front. The skirt or trousers are often designed to be comfortable and modest.

Malay Dance

Joget

Joget is a traditional Malay dance that originated in Malaysia. It is known for its lively and energetic movements, often performed to upbeat and rhythmic music. The dance typically involves a series of fast, rhythmic steps and hand movements, and it is usually performed by couples or groups.

Zapin

Zapin is characterized by its elegant and rhythmic movements, performed to traditional music that typically features drums, gongs, and other percussion instruments. The dance is usually performed by a group of dancers, often in a semi-circle or line formation. The movements are coordinated and involve intricate footwork, hand gestures, and body sways.

Chinese Tradition

Chinese Costume

Cheongsam

Cheongsam is a Chinese dress worn by women. It is most often seen as a longer, figure-fitting, one piece garment with a standing collar, an asymmetric, left-over-right opening and two side slits, and embellished with Chinese frog fasteners on the lapel and the collar.

Tang Suit

Tang suit is a traditional Chinese attire that typically features a high collar and a distinctive button-up design. It often includes a jacket and matching trousers. The jacket is usually adorned with intricate patterns or embroidery and fastened with knot buttons or loops.

Hanfu

Hanfu typically features a flowing, loose-fitting design with a wrap-around style. It usually consists of a top garment paired with a skirt or trousers, and it may include additional layers or accessories like sashes and belts.

Mao Suit

Mao suit features a straight, button-up jacket with a high collar, and it is usually worn with matching trousers. It typically has four pockets, two on the chest and two on the lower part of the jacket. It is made from durable, often dark-colored fabric.

Chinese Dance

Dragon Dance

Dragon dance is a traditional Chinese performance that is often featured during celebrations such as Chinese New Year, the Lantern Festival, and other festive events. The dance involves a team of performers who manipulate a long, flexible dragon costume to mimic the movements of a dragon, which is a revered symbol in Chinese culture representing strength, power, and good fortune.

Lion Dance

Lion dance is a traditional Chinese performance that is often featured during celebrations like Chinese New Year. The lion costume is typically made of brightly colored, elaborate fabric and is worn by two performers. The costume consists of a head and a body, and the performers work together to move the lion with one controlling the head and the other managing the body.

Indian Tradition

Indian Costume

Saree

Saree is a women's attire from the Indian. It consists of an un-stitched stretch of woven fabric arranged over the body as a robe with one end attached to the waist. While the other end rests over one shoulder as a stole, sometimes baring a part of the midriff.

Sherwani

Sherwani is a traditional, long coat-like attire worn by men. It is a formal attire that is often associated with weddings, ceremonies, and other special occasions. It is a knee-length or longer coat that is fitted closely to the body. In terms of, it has a front opening with buttons and is usually tailored to give a sharp and elegant contour.

Indian Dance

Bharatanatyam

Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest and most classical dance forms of India. It is known for its grace, precision and a deep connection to spirituality and storytelling. It is characterized by fixed upper torsos, bent legs (known as Araimandi), intricate footwork and expressive hand gestures (mudras).

Kathak

Kathak is a dance from northern India and it is often a dance of love. It is performed by both men and women. The movements include intricate footwork accented by bells worn around the ankles and stylized gestures adapted from normal body language.

Others Tradition

Costume

Koubasanan

Koubasanan is the traditional costume of the Kadazan-Dusun. It is made out of black velvet fabric with various decorations using beads, flowers, coloured buttons, golden laces, linen and unique embroidery designs.

Baju Burung

Baju Burung refers to a traditional ceremonial attire worn by the Iban people. It is typically made from handwoven fabric and may include intricate beadwork, embroidery and feathers, particularly from birds like the hornbill which holds special significance in Iban culture.

Dance

Sumazau

Sumazau is a traditional dance of the Kadazan-Dusun. The dance is often performed in pairs, with men and women facing each other. The arms are held out to the sides at shoulder height with a slight bend in the elbows and the hands move in a gentle, rhythmic motion that mimics the flight of birds, particularly the eagle.

Ngajat

Ngajat is one of the most iconic and culturally significant dances among the Iban people. It is characterized by its strong and graceful movements, which often mimic the actions of a warrior. The dancers may perform slow and deliberate steps or more dynamic movements depending on the variation of the dance.
