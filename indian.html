Indian Cuisine

Banana Leaf Rice

Originating from Southern India, banana leaf rice is a traditional way of serving meals where rice is placed on a banana leaf, accompanied by various vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. Ingredients include steamed rice, curries, vegetables, pickles, and papadums.

Nasi Kandar

Nasi Kandar is a popular Malaysian dish with origins in the Indian-Muslim. The name "kandar" refers to the traditional method of carrying containers of rice and side dishes using a bamboo pole. This dish features steamed rice served with an assortment of flavorful curries, gravies, and accompaniments such as fried chicken, beef, fish, prawns, and vegetables.

❮ ❯

Nasi Briyani

Originating from South Asia, Nasi Briyani is a fragrant rice dish infused with spices like saffron, cardamom, and cinnamon. It often includes meat like chicken or mutton, cooked with basmati rice.
